# Consultation Pitch Scripts

AI-generated pitch to convert intake callers into booked consultations.

## Voice Delivery Notes

The pitches below are 2–3 paragraphs, which is intentionally longer than normal intake turns. However, **deliver them in two parts** with a natural pause for the caller to respond:

- **Part 1:** The hook (1–2 sentences describing the attorney fit). Deliver, then pause.
- **Part 2:** The offer + call-to-action. Deliver only after the caller has acknowledged Part 1.

This prevents a 30-second monologue with no chance for the caller to react. If the caller interrupts at any point, treat that as a barge-in and respond to what they said.

Also: fill in all `[bracketed placeholders]` at deploy time or inject dynamically at runtime. Never speak a bracketed placeholder literally.

## English Pitch

Use after enough intake information is collected (typically after Phase 5 — case-specific questions).

### Standard Pitch (most case types)

> "Based on what you've shared with me today, it sounds like [ATTORNEY NAME] would be a great fit to review your situation. [He/She] specializes in [case type — e.g., family immigration / work visas / asylum cases] and has helped many families just like yours.
>
> We offer a [free / $X] initial consultation where [he/she] will go through your specific situation in detail, answer all your questions, and outline the best path forward for your case.
>
> The consultation is [30 / 60] minutes, and we can do it over the phone or in-person at our office. Spaces fill up quickly — would you like me to find you the first available time this week or next?"

### Urgent Case Pitch (court date, expiring visa)

> "Given that you have [a court date / a visa expiring] [soon / in X weeks], it's really important to speak with an attorney as quickly as possible. The sooner we can sit down and review your options, the more options you'll have available.
>
> I have an expedited slot available as early as [tomorrow / this week]. Can we secure that for you right now?"

### Detention / Emergency (use only if transfer is not available)

> "I understand this is an emergency situation with your [husband/wife/family member] being detained. Our attorneys handle detention cases urgently.
>
> I'm going to book you the very first available slot — which is [date/time]. In the meantime, I'll flag this as urgent so our team is prepared when you arrive. Does that work?"

### Returning Caller (no new consultation needed — just reengagement)

> "It's great to hear from you again. Since we last spoke, [ATTORNEY NAME] has been [working on cases like yours / keeping up with changes in the law that could affect your case].
>
> Would you like to schedule a follow-up to discuss where things stand and any new options that may be available?"

## Handling Pushback

**"I need to think about it"**
> "Of course, I completely understand. Immigration decisions are important and you want to be sure.
>
> The consultation itself is [free / low-cost] and there's no obligation to move forward. It's really just a chance to get clarity on your options from an expert.
>
> Would it help if I held a slot for you for the next 24 hours while you think it over?"

**"I can't afford an attorney"**
> "I understand that cost is a concern — many of our clients feel the same way.
>
> Our attorneys offer flexible payment plans and can work with you on fees based on your situation. The initial consultation is [free / just $X], so there's no commitment up front.
>
> Immigration issues often get more complicated and more expensive the longer they wait. Let's at least get you a clear picture of where you stand — would that be okay?"

**"I already have an attorney"**
> "No problem at all! If you ever find you'd like a second opinion, or if your current situation changes, we're always here.
>
> Is there anything I can help you with today, or would you like me to note your information for a future follow-up?"

**"I'm not ready"**
> "That's completely okay. May I take down your best contact information and reach out in [1 week / 30 days] to check in?
>
> Immigration timelines can be unpredictable, and we want to make sure you have support when you need it."

## Spanish Pitch

### Discurso Estándar

> "Según lo que me ha compartido hoy, parece que [NOMBRE DEL ABOGADO/A] sería la persona ideal para revisar su situación. [Él/Ella] se especializa en [tipo de caso — ej. inmigración familiar / visas de trabajo / casos de asilo] y ha ayudado a muchas familias como la suya.
>
> Ofrecemos una consulta inicial [gratuita / por $X] en la que [él/ella] revisará su situación específica en detalle, responderá todas sus preguntas y le indicará el mejor camino a seguir para su caso.
>
> La consulta dura [30 / 60] minutos y puede ser por teléfono o en persona en nuestra oficina. Los horarios se llenan rápido — ¿le busco el primer tiempo disponible esta semana o la próxima?"

### Discurso Urgente

> "Dado que tiene [una audiencia / una visa que vence] [pronto / en X semanas], es muy importante hablar con un abogado lo antes posible. Cuanto antes podamos revisar sus opciones, más posibilidades tendrá disponibles.
>
> Tengo un horario expedito disponible tan pronto como [mañana / esta semana]. ¿Lo aseguramos ahora mismo?"

### Manejo de Objeciones en Español

**"Tengo que pensarlo"**
> "Por supuesto, lo entiendo perfectamente. Las decisiones migratorias son importantes.
>
> La consulta en sí es [gratuita / muy accesible] y no hay ningún compromiso. Es simplemente una oportunidad para aclarar sus opciones con un experto.
>
> ¿Le ayudaría si le reservo un horario por las próximas 24 horas mientras lo piensa?"

**"No puedo pagar un abogado"**
> "Entiendo que el costo es una preocupación — muchos de nuestros clientes se sienten igual.
>
> Nuestros abogados ofrecen planes de pago flexibles y pueden trabajar con usted según su situación. La consulta inicial es [gratuita / solo $X], sin ningún compromiso por adelantado.
>
> Los problemas migratorios suelen complicarse más y costar más mientras más se espera. Al menos permítanos darle un panorama claro de su situación — ¿estaría bien?"

## Booking Confirmation Phrases

After the caller agrees to book:

**English:**
> "Perfect! I have you down for [Day, Date] at [Time] with [Attorney Name]. You'll receive a text confirmation shortly.
>
> Please try to have [relevant documents] ready if you can — but don't worry if you don't have everything. The attorney will guide you through what you'll need.
>
> Is there anything else I can help you with today?"

**Spanish:**
> "¡Perfecto! Le he programado para el [Día, Fecha] a las [Hora] con [Nombre del Abogado/a]. Recibirá una confirmación por mensaje de texto en breve.
>
> Si puede, tenga listos [documentos relevantes] — pero no se preocupe si no tiene todo. El/La abogado/a le indicará lo que necesitará.
>
> ¿Hay algo más en lo que pueda ayudarle hoy?"
